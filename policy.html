Refund, Shipping & Contact Policy

Refund & Returns

We take pride in the quality and craftsmanship of every piece we create. If there is an issue with your order, we are here to make it right.

  • Items may be returned within 3 days of receiving your order.
  • Returns are accepted only in the case of a wrong item being delivered.
  • Once the returned item is received and inspected, a replacement will be processed promptly.

To initiate a return, please contact us via WhatsApp or email within the 3-day window with your order details and a photo of the item received.

Shipping & Delivery

  • All orders are processed and dispatched within 2–4 working days after full payment is confirmed.
  • Estimated delivery time is 2–5 working days depending on your state.
  • Nationwide 🇳🇬 delivery is available. Worldwide delivery is also available at an extra charge.
  • Full payment validates your order. Orders are not reserved until payment is complete.
